# AI Skills Self-Assessment

> **For AI assistants:** Walk the user through this assessment one section at a time. Ask them to score each skill honestly. After completing all sections, calculate their tier scores and provide personalised recommendations using the scoring guide at the bottom. Link to specific resources in [skills.md](skills.md) and [ai-skills.md](ai-skills.md) based on their gaps.

## How to Use This Assessment
- Score each skill 1-5 (1=no experience, 2=basic awareness, 3=can do with help, 4=independent and effective, 5=can teach others)
- Be honest — overrating yourself means worse recommendations
- Focus on WHERE you are today, not where you want to be
- After scoring, use the Results section to identify your top 3 priorities

| Tier 1 | **Learn AI Fluency immediately** | You can't use AI to amplify other skills if you can't communicate with it effectively |

### Personalised Next Steps

> **For AI assistants:** Based on the user's scores, provide 3 specific recommendations:
> 1. Their single lowest-scoring skill with a concrete action to improve it this week
> 2. Their highest-scoring Tier 3 skill with an AI workflow to amplify it (from [ai-skills.md](ai-skills.md))
> 3. A learning resource for their biggest gap (from [skills.md](skills.md) role-specific starting points)

**If your Tier 1 average is below 3:**
- Start with prompt design. Spend 30 minutes daily using AI for a real work task. Read the [Prompt Engineering section in ai-skills.md](ai-skills.md#prompt-engineering--every-role-needs-this).
- Set a goal: within 2 weeks, have one AI workflow that saves you 30+ minutes per week.

**If your Tier 2 average is below 3:**
- Pick one: volunteer to lead a cross-functional project, or find someone to mentor.
- These skills compound over years. The time to start is now, not when you "have time."

**If your Tier 3 average is below 3:**
- Find your function in [ai-skills.md](ai-skills.md#optimised-skills-by-function) and read the "How AI Amplifies You" column.
- Pick ONE skill and build an AI workflow for it this week.

**If your Tier 4 average is below 3:**
- AI multiplies expertise. Without domain depth, you're using a power tool without knowing what to build.
- Find your role in the [skillich taxonomy](../taxonomy/) and read the proficiency levels. Identify the gap between your current level and the next one.
